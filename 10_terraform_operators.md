## Arithmetic and Logical Operators
- Arithmetic `+` `-` `*` `/` `%`
- Equality `==` `!=`
- Comparison `<` `<=` `>` `>=`
- Logical `||` `&&` `!`

### Order of operations
> Use Parentheses () to override the default order of operations.
1. `!` `-` (*multiplied by -1*)
2. `*` `/` `%`
3. `+` `-` (*subtract*)
4. `>` `>=` `<` `<=`
5. `==` `!=`
6. `&&`
7. `||`
